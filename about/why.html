Why rely on us

Why rely on us

1. Expert Knowledge and Totally Tailor-Made Experiences

We offer truly individual luxury travel experiences to Rajasthan. Tailor-made to the finest detail with carefully-chosen hotels to match your style. Offering first-hand expert advice with impeccable service from your first point of contact to when you return home, we create an itinerary for you, beginning from scratch, to suit your interests. We ensure everyone is happy and all the right boxes are ticked be it a family holiday, a first time trip to India or a leisurely journey across Rajasthan. 

 

2. With Age Comes Trust and Experience

With 60 years under our belt of organising luxury travel, we have built great long-standing relationships with the very best hotels, guides and travel partners. We are serious experts in careful planning and handling, leading to exciting and effortless holidays that bring Rajasthan and other parts of India to life.

 

3. Excellent On-the-Ground Support

We do not outsource. We have offices across India and our very own experienced drivers and guides. Every little detail is checked. Our drivers have worked for us for more than 30 years. They are reliable and knowledgeable, offering wonderful service and companionship throughout your holiday. They are well-versed in the safest and fastest routes which have come with years of driving experience on the winding roads of India’s motorways
 

4. Creative and Innovative Experiences

India is constantly evolving. You need someone who lives in the heart of Rajasthan and knows the region inside out. We are very up-to-date with the opening of new hotels and constantly monitor the quality of existing ones, ensuring that your holiday is seamless. We continually introduce unique tailor-made off-the-beaten-track experiences so you have an immersive experience of India.

 

5. Boutique and Luxury hotels

Rajasthan has a thrilling blend of luxury, boutique, heritage and affordable hotels as well as luxury camps. While the luxury sector is magical, it’s not only about opulence. We have brilliant working relationships with every type of hotel at great prices so that your holiday is affordable wherever you decide to stay.

 

6. Approved by India’s Ministry of Tourism

We are members of the Indian Association of Travel Agents and approved by the Ministry of Tourism, a branch of the Government of India.
